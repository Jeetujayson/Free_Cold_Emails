Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2nd October 2023

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect the following personal information from users:
Email addresses, Names, Contact information.
This information is collected through our signup form for account creation.

Additionally, users may choose to:
Link their email addresses for sending emails, Load email lists of their target audience

2. Storage and Protection of User Data

We store user data in a PostgreSQL database. We take measures to ensure the security of this data, including encryption and secure server protocols.

3. Sharing of User Data

We may sell user data for advertising purposes.

4. User Access, Update, and Deletion

Users can access, update, or delete their personal information through their account settings.

5. Use of Cookies

We use cookies on our website for tracking and analytics purposes.

6. Email Handling

Emails are sent through users' email service providers' SMTP servers and received through IMAP.

7. Opt-Out

Users can either use the option to opt-out of email communications via an unsubscribe link or by manually removing recipients from their list.

8. Data Retention

We retain user data for 7 days.

9. CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act.
